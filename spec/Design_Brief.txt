

## 1) General design description

A premium, calm, editorial UI built around **warm parchment neutrals + muted brass accents** so the **Rider–Waite card colors stay the hero**. The layout is very clean: a light “paper” app shell, an airy spread stage with a *barely-there pressed-paper texture*, and a chat area separated by soft elevation and rounded corners. Buttons and icons are “quiet” (thin strokes, low contrast), with just enough hierarchy through spacing, hairlines, and subtle shadows.

**Overall vibe:** museum label / book paper / quiet luxury.

## 4) Background texture spec (the “very slight elegant texture”)

**Type:** *Pressed paper / parchment grain* (not noisy, not “grunge”).
Think: smooth cotton paper with a subtle fiber + emboss variation.

**Implementation suggestion (CSS):**

* Base stage is flat color.
* Add **2 overlays** at very low opacity:

  1. **Fine grain** (tiny monochrome noise) at **3–5% opacity**
  2. **Soft emboss blotches** (large, blurred cloud texture) at **2–4% opacity**

**Target look:** You notice it only when you look for it; it prevents the spread stage from feeling “dead flat”.

---

## 5) Typography (fonts)

This design reads best with a **high-quality editorial serif** for headers + a **clean modern sans** for chat.

### Recommended pairing A (premium, easy to ship)

* **Headers / spread title:** `Fraunces` (Google Font)

  * Weight: 500–600
  * Letter spacing: +0.08em (small caps vibe if you want)
* **Body / chat / UI:** `Inter`

  * Weight: 400–500

### Alternative pairing B (even more “book”)

* Headers: `Cormorant Garamond` (600)
* Body: `Source Sans 3` (400–500)

**Type colors**

* Primary: `#2F2A24`
* Secondary: `#6B6157`

---

## 6) Layout & separation rules (what makes it feel refined)

* Separation is done via:

  * **Hairline dividers** (`#D8CFC1`)
  * **One layer of elevation** (chat panel sits “above” the page)
  * **Spacing** (more whitespace, fewer boxes)
* Keep icon buttons in header **small + outlined**, not filled, so the content stays dominant.
* Keep the spread stage **quiet**: texture + maybe a faint frame, but no gradient drama.

---

## 7) Quick sizing (optional but practical)

* Corner radius:

  * Chat bubbles: 16–20
  * Input field: 14–16
  * Icon buttons: 12–14
* Hairline: 1px at ~60% opacity
* Spacing:

  * Header padding: 16–20
  * Spread stage padding: 24+
  * Chat padding: 16–20

---

Notes for implementation (important)

Do NOT add gradients beyond extremely soft vignettes.

Texture should be implemented as a low-opacity overlay, never baked into the base color.

Rider–Waite cards must cast shadows to feel tactile against the paper.

Avoid pure white anywhere — it breaks the illusion.
